Symbiosis Institute of Technology, Nagpur

Mission is to inculcate spirit of "Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam" (the world is one family)

AAGMAN 2K24

↓

DAY 1: Inauguration

On the first day of college we were introduced to our Director sir, Academic Incharge, DSRW, SIU Library Facilities, also about Mentor-Mentee Scheme. Then after lunch break we went for exploring our college campus with our senior buddies. It was fun to explore the campus.

DAY 2: Know About SIT and SRC (Student's Representative Council)

We were given an introduction to the international, training and placement, and entrepreneurial cells in the morning. and following lunch, we learned about the members of the many clubs in the SIT  as well as the SRC.

DAY 3: INDUSTRY CONCLAVE

On this day, we learned about emerging trends and technologies, industry-academic collaboration, sustainable industrial practices, and skill development and employment options by the renowned visitor from HCL Tech, Tech Mahindra, Infosys, Global Logic, Infocepts, TCS, and Delaplex organizations.

DAY 4: Research and Innovation

On this day, we learned about intellectual property rights. Details about patent filing and industrial design filing. also the value of research in academics by Dr. shyam kumar Barik, the deputy controller of patents and designs, Patent office & RGNIIPM - Govt. of India.

DAY 5: Community Connect and Social Responsibility Awareness

Workshop on "empowering youth leadership in social work." The speech by Mr. Piyush Boinwar Sir and Mr. Nilesh Seenakke Sir on "I Am Creator of My Own Life" was undoubtedly inspirational.

DAY 6: Enterpreneurship

The day was focused about the entrepreneurial spirit. The true success tales of entrepreneurs, in their own words. Everyone on the dice directed the kids in such a lovely way, telling us about the hardships they had and the guru-mantras they followed.

  • Mr. Mithilesh Vazalwar [Founder, Corridor Seven Coffee Roasters]
  • Mr. Aliasger Wagh [Partner, Royal Packaging]
  • Mr. Parimal Kalikar [Founder, Licksters]
  • Mr. Chirag Pughaliya [Founder, Mater Media]
  • and many more..

DAY 7: Fitness

We began the morning with a Zumba session, which I thoroughly enjoyed. We had a great time doing Zumba in the light drizzle.
Following that, we had a session on grooming and eating habits conducted by Mrs. Veena Bahekar, food and hygiene specialist.

DAY 8: Sports

Earlier in the morning, we discussed the importance of team bonding. After that, we were split up into teams, assigned interesting projects to complete, and the winners were declared. After lunch, we played sports. And then we were told to run/walk for 12-15 minutes before taking a fitness test.

DAY 9: Profiling

First we had a seminar on moodle [ERP] and Codetantra. Then, professor Monali Guhahne Mam (social media & branding incharge) conducted student profiles on a variety of social media sites, including Instagram, HackerRank, Github, LinkedIn, FaceBook, etc.

DAY 10: Cultural Activities by Freshers

Finally, the day arrives when all freshers perform a group dance,solo dance, singing,and drama on stage. It was a blast to do this. We had an outstanding day. It was the best out of all ten days. This marked the end of our AAGMAN 2K24.
